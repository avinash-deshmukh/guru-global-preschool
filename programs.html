Programs

Age-appropriate programs for every early learner.

From Playgroup to Senior KG, our structured yet playful programs are carefully designed to meet the developmental needs of each age group while nurturing confidence, language, and life skills.

Playgroup

Playgroup (Age 2–3)

Gentle introduction to school with lots of free play, music, rhymes, and sensory experiences. Children learn to separate smoothly from parents and enjoy a happy, structured routine.

Nursery

Nursery (Age 3–4)

Focus on building early language, number sense, fine motor skills, and social interactions through stories, songs, hands-on activities, and circle time.

Junior KG

Junior KG (Age 4–5)

Strengthens pre-reading, writing, and math skills with phonics, pre-writing strokes, early number operations, theme-based projects, and group activities.

Senior KG

Senior KG (Age 5–6)

Holistic school readiness program that focuses on reading, writing, numeracy, communication, and independence to smoothly transition children into Grade 1.

Key Features Across All Programs

  • • Small group sizes for personal attention.
  • • Play-based, activity-rich learning experiences.
  • • Strong focus on language, social skills, and values.
  • • Regular communication and updates for parents.

Looking for additional classes?

We also offer specialized programs like Abacus Classes (Age 5+), Phonics Classes (Age 3+), Vedic Maths (Age 13+), and Handwriting Classes (Age 5+). These help strengthen specific skills beyond regular school hours.
