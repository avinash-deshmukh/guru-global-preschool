Admissions

A simple, friendly admission process.

We keep the admission process easy and transparent so that your family can focus on what matters most—your child’s smooth transition into preschool.

  1. 1

    Fill enquiry form

    Share your child's details, age, and preferred program (Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG) so we can guide you better.

  2. 2

    School visit

    Visit Guru Global Preschool at Bungalow C, Lodha Regency, Diva Manpada Road, Dombivli East to see classrooms, interact with educators, and clarify all your questions.

  3. 3

    Submit documents

    Provide the required documents and complete the admission form as guided by our team at the school.

  4. 4

    Confirm admission

    Confirm your child's seat by completing fee formalities and receive the welcome kit and joining details.

Need help with admissions?

Our team is happy to help you understand which program suits your child best and assist with any queries about timings, fees, and transport.
