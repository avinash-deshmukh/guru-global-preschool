Preschool Near Lodha Regency & Nearby Areas

Guru Global Preschool is a preschool and day care center in Lodha Regency, Bhadra Nagar, Dombivli East on Diva Manpada Road. Our school welcomes children from nearby residential communities including Lodha Panacea, Lodha Park, Runwal Gardens, Runwal MyCity, Desale Pada, and Gokuldham Complex. We offer Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG, and Day Care programs along with after-school enrichment classes such as Abacus, Phonics, Handwriting Improvement, and Vedic Maths to help children build strong foundations in learning and confidence.
